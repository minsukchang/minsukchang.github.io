Minsuk Chang

Research Scientist at Naver AI Lab.

Hi :) I am a research scientist at NAVER AI Lab, where I explore novel computational interaction techniques powered by AI technologies. I’m also a technical leader at NAVER Clova, where I lead a team of engineers for building and evaluating AI powered systems with users in the loop.

My current research interests are 1) modeling conversational interactions, 2) making massive scale language models usable and useful, and 3) simulation-based models of users and interfaces.

I have a PhD in Computer Science from KAIST. During my PhD, I was advised by Juho Kim in the KIXLAB.
