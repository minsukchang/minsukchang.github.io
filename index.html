Bio

I am a research scientist at Google DeepMind. I study the mechanisms that enable biological and artificial agents to learn and adapt through interaction. My current work centers on post-training methodologies for Gemini and generative multi-agent simulations (Concordia).

The ACM named me an Asian American and Pacific Islander Pioneer and Luminary in 2023. I hold a PhD from KAIST.
